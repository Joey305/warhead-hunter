Where The Output Is Most Useful

PRO

PROTAC Linker Inspection

Inspect which atoms on a target-binding ligand remain solvent exposed before proposing linker attachment hypotheses.

WAR

Covalent Warhead Exploration

Review exposed ligand positions that may support further chemical elaboration or covalent-warhead design.

EXP

Chemical Expansion

Identify ligand regions that remain accessible and may tolerate substituent growth.

CMP

Comparative PDB Analysis

Compare ligand exposure patterns across related structures, ligands, chains, or binding modes.

EDU

Medicinal Chemistry Education

Use synchronized 2D and 3D views to teach how ligand burial and solvent exposure influence modification-site selection.
