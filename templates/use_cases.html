Warhead Hunter Use Cases

Warhead Hunter is designed for structure-guided medicinal chemistry workflows in which ligand atom exposure can help frame candidate modification-site inspection.

1. PROTAC Linker Attachment-Site Inspection

Use Warhead Hunter to inspect which atoms on a target-binding ligand remain solvent exposed in a bound structure before proposing linker attachment hypotheses.

2. Covalent Inhibitor Warhead Exploration

Review exposed ligand positions that may support further chemical elaboration or covalent-warhead design.

3. Chemical Expansion of Protein-Bound Ligands

Identify ligand regions that remain accessible and may tolerate substituent growth.

4. Comparative Ligand Analysis Across PDB Structures

Compare ligand exposure patterns across related structures, ligands, chains, or binding modes.

5. Structure-Guided Medicinal Chemistry Education

Use synchronized 2D and 3D views to teach how ligand burial and solvent exposure influence modification-site selection.
